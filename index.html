About Me

Bo-Yuan Deng is a research assistant in the Department of English at the National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology in Taiwan. He graduated from the Department of English at the National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology, specializing in Applied Linguistics and TESOL. His research interests include computer-assisted language learning (CALL), approaches to English as a Second/Foreign Language (ESL/EFL), and Applied Linguistics.

M.A.: Applied Linguistics and TESOL, NKUST, Kaohsiung, Taiwan

B.A.: Department of English, NKUST, Kaohsiung, Taiwan

Senior High: Language Acquiring & Cross-Cultural Learning, Marsden High School, Sydney, Australia
